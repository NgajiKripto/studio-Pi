# **App Name**: Zepret

## Core Features:

- Real-time Camera Access & Preview: Utilizes WebRTC for direct browser camera access, providing a live mirrored preview for capturing photos.
- Interactive Customization Editor: Allows users to select and apply various frames and effects, and to drag, scale, and rotate 2D and 3D sticker assets onto their live photo preview.
- Timed Photo Session Management: Manages the countdown timer for each photo session package, facilitating multiple captures within the allocated time.
- Monetization & Payment Gateway Integration: Enables package selection (e.g., 5 or 10 minutes) and purchasing premium customization items through an integrated payment gateway like Midtrans/Xendit.
- Generated Photo Export & Sharing: Provides options to generate final photo layouts (e.g., grid, strip), download high-resolution images (with watermark removal post-payment), and share directly to social media.
- AI-Powered Style Assistant Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests trending or personalized frame designs and sticker arrangements based on user preferences or photo context, enhancing the Neobrutalism aesthetic.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A bold and vibrant pink (#FC5988), reflecting a dominant aesthetic for Gen Z and Neobrutalism.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated pink (#FCF4F5) providing a subtle base that complements the primary hue.
- Accent color: A strong, energetic yellow (#F2EB08) to create sharp, contrasting focal points consistent with Neobrutalism's vibrant accent style.
- High contrast elements: Utilize crisp black (#111) for borders, text, and shadows, along with pure white (#FFF) for clean backgrounds and typography contrast.
- Heading font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for bold, uppercase headlines with a modern, techy Neobrutalism feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, high legibility body text, ensuring readability while maintaining a contemporary aesthetic.
- Icons: Characterized by thick borders, bold forms, and strong geometric shapes to align with the Neobrutalism aesthetic.
- Visual style: Features thick borders (3-4px), harsh box shadows, and an asymmetrical yet balanced layout, with a mobile-first approach ensuring optimal display across devices.
- Micro-interactions: Incorporate engaging micro-interactions such as 'hover bounce' effects, an audible 'snap' sound on capture, a pulsating timer, and celebratory confetti upon session completion.
